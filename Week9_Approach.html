What does this map show?

In college I spent four years on the alpine ski and sailing club sports teams traveling around the Pacific Northwest and beyond. I met some of my closest friends on these teams and I was able to see a large part of the West Coast through my travels. Every trip except two were taken in 12 passenger vans, driving from three to 18 hours at a time! This map shows where I traveled for each year of college, by clicking the buttons above you will move from place to place in the order of trips taken.
